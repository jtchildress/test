Australian Grand Prix

About the race

The Australian Grand Prix was the first race of the season. This was the real big show of the testing and the engineering of the cars. It was the first example of who would be leading the season and who would be the runner-ups because unlike other seasons most of the cars would not be upgraded after the Imalia Grand Prix.
